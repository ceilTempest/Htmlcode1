MSc Computer Science is a two-year post-graduation program to develop advanced programming skills and strategies to solve various logical challenges using different programming languages. MSc Computer Science or MSc CS focuses more on developing software and networking-based skills.

The objective of the two-year MSc Computer Science curriculum is to develop advanced programming skills and approaches to address a variety of logical problems utilising various programming languages. The foundation, core, electives, and projects sections of the M.Sc. Computer Science degree curriculum are divided into four semesters. The focus of MSc Computer Science, also known as MSc CS, is focused on the development of software and networking-based abilities. After earning an M.Sc. in Computer Science, there are numerous job options available, including those in networking, software development and testing, web development, cryptography, etc.
